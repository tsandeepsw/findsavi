Instructions

First, you have to guess ‘n’ (a randomly generated number between 1 and 99). When your guess matches ‘n’, the game is over.

If not, an addition or subtraction operation will be performed to generate a ‘resultant’ that falls between 1 and 99. Thereafter, you must guess the resultant.

If you fail to guess the resultant correctly, another addition or subtraction operation will be performed to generate a new resultant. Use the clues provided in the results section to figure out the resultant!
